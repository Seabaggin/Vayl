Desire Map · the full flow

Rate privately → discover where you and your partner align → unlock the shared map. The reveal is the app's primary paywall moment. Your answers become stars; your overlap becomes the constellation. Two views of one map: the constellation (felt) and the list (legible, also the Vault).

branch by who finishes second

Finishing is a transition, not a screen ("your map is charted"). The second finisher resolves straight into the reveal (the gift). The first finisher lands on their own summary while waiting, and on re-entry it carries a bright "reveal ready" bar. Wait earns the anticipation; completing the pair earns the payoff.

reveal = a sheet model

Two views of one map. Tap a star → its detail sheet (locked → paywall). Top-right Full map → the matches as expandable cards. That list is the Vault view, build it once. Pre-unlock both views gate identically (free readable, locked blurred, one CTA).

mutual-only · the intention

Only desires both marked surface; notForMe syncs but never surfaces to your partner (excluded from every shared view), probablyNot is a soft no. Mutual vs adjacent ("worth exploring", one yes + one curious) read differently. Discover shared desire without the risk of going first.

Swift-native — flag, do not mock

⚑Finish transition (both done) — the personal sky's matching stars converge and light into the shared constellation. The metaphor payoff; built on device.
⚑Star = cross + sparkle twinkle — resting cross with an occasional sparkle ping (keyframeAnimator). Shown at rest here.
⚑Sheets in the cover — detail / Full map / PaywallSheet host inside one .vaylCover(confirmOnExit:false), unlock in place.

Edge states (noted, not drawn)

Already-Core couple: skip the paywall, whole sky lit as a celebration. 0 matches: graceful empty. 1 match: free star only, gentle close, no gap.

Token mapping

void · warm desire glow magenta→purple · DesireRatingValue 4-point · DesireMatchType mutual/adjacent · spectrum chrome · PaywallSheet(entry:.reveal) · .vaylCover · the list reused in Map tab / Vault.
