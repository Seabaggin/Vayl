Couple Session
North-Star Register

Three directions for the in-person, two-device session. Same prompt across all three cards — you're comparing register, not content. Calmer and lower-chrome than OB; eyes-up, set-the-phone-down.

01 Ember Void

Palette
Pure void, one low magenta aurora pooling at the base like candlelight off-frame. Spectrum rationed to a single hairline.
Chrome
Almost none. The prompt floats unhoused — no card, no container. Lowest possible budget.
Register
Hushed, candlelit, intimate. The screen recedes so you two are what's lit.

02 Glass Slab

Palette
Void plus one frosted glass plane over a soft purple wash. Spectrum lives on the slab's top edge.
Chrome
One surface, only one. The card becomes a held object you pass between you. Most literally buildable.
Register
Composed, grounded — a space you're holding together. The safest, most legible.

03 Spectrum Thread

Palette
Barest void, aurora near zero. Colour concentrated into one continuous cyan→magenta thread.
Chrome
No surfaces. One line does every job — progress, turn cue, the hold ring. Authored by a single gesture.
Register
A live wire between two people. Alive and connected, still calm. Most signature.
