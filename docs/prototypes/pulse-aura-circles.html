The aura, just a circle

No highlight, centered light, clear color. Breathing. Four small variations on "a glowing circle that emits rather than reflects."

1 · Emission
Bright center fading to nothing. Most ethereal, softest edge.
2 · Core + halo
Even, solid body with a soft glow ring. Clearest, most saturated color.
3 · Soft orb
The original, de-planeted: centered volume, no glossy highlight.
4 · Inner rim
Even body with a glowing inner edge. Dimension without a fake light source.

My lean: 2 (Core + halo) or 1 (Emission). Both read as light, hold the tier color cleanly, and scale to a tiny widget dot without losing meaning. 3 is the safest "it's just the orb, fixed." 4 adds a touch of dimension if 2 feels too flat.
