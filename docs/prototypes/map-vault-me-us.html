The map does the Path move

Your map (Me, private ratings) becomes Where you meet (Us, the overlap). Same data, two honest lenses: rank-your-own on Me, compare-two-points on Us. The Desire Map reveal is what lights the Us shelf.

One journal, two doors

A note written on a Path node lands in Your journal, tagged to that landmark (see the pinned note). No second journal. You can also write freeform here. Private always, until you choose to share.

It's a 1D Pulse

The orb, the aura, and the "2 weeks ago" ghost all reuse the Pulse engine (position model + history overlay). This is why it's the near-free one to build, and it stitches the journal to the hero visually.

How the mechanics live here: each is a kind of moment, not a separate feature. Between two pulls is a 1D Pulse (reuses the aura + position-over-time overlay). What's starved reuses the rater's drag + pills, and stays a mirror ("here's how you ranked today", never a type). Two voices is a split layout. The Erasure is the distillation step that turns a paragraph into the one italic sentence you see sealed in the timeline: it powers "Just write" and the Jealousy Map's core, it is not its own tile. Everything is private, dated, and kept, while the standing map stays separate and permanent. A sealed moment surfaces when you return, never as a notification.
The fork for you: this shows the Vault as a compact block that pushes into a full screen. The alternative is heavier inline rows on the dashboard (more present, but the tab gets tall under the Pulse hero + Path). I lean compact-and-push for calm. Also confirm: lean means no "saved articles / decks" shelf, the Vault holds what you made together, not a reading list. Say if you want the record given more room than a shelf, since it folded in from the old Me layer.
