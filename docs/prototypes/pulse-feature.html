Two-line capacity

Primary is your own capacity over time, a data mirror of your check-ins. Secondary is a toggle that overlays your partner's line so you can see the gap between your two capacities.

The graph language
  • Smooth line — flowing capacity curve, spectrum styling, no jagged EKG.
  • You = solid · partner = dashed + their initial. Color is decorative and identical for both; it encodes nothing.
  • Neutral tier zones — E / S / F / P bands as a quiet backdrop, not colored grid lines.
  • Tap a dot → that day's check-in summary.
  • Shared date axis — both lines connect their own check-ins by date, last ~7.
The read
  • In compare, the read is the gap between the two lines.
  • Shown, never interpreted — no "in sync" / "drifting" labels, no advice. A neutral mirror.
