VAYL

Circles, pushed

Three of Emission, three of Core + halo. Risk in what the light does, not in the silhouette.

Emission
E1 · Spectrum bloom
Cyan core, but the outer halo bleeds into purple then magenta. Vayl spectrum at the edge.
E2 · Living caustic
Light moves inside it, like sun through water. Never still, never repeats.
E3 · Pulse ring
A ring of light expands out and fades on a slow beat. Literally a pulse.
Core + halo
C1 · Spectrum halo
Solid clear cyan body, the glow around it runs the full spectrum.
C2 · Traveling glint
A single bright catch of light slowly orbits the rim. Alive, not a lit planet.
C3 · Void center
Light is a luminous shell; the void shows through the middle. A lens, not a coin.

My picks to push further: E2 (living caustic) is the most alive and the most unmistakably Vayl, light behaving like light. C3 (void center) is the boldest, it makes the aura a window into the void rather than an object, which is very on-brand and very different from a ball. E1 / C1 (spectrum) are the safe-but-strong picks if you want the brand spectrum present.
