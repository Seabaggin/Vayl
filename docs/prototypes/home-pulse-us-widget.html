Showing both of you on the widget

Four ways to put you + your partner in the same compact pane. All use a "wide day" example (you Expansive/cyan, Alex Protective/rose) so the contrast is visible. The goal: the composition should say close-or-far at a glance — motion/position that means something, not orbs bouncing for decoration.

Two orbs joined by a thin thread that stretches on a wide day and pulls tight when you share a space. The distance IS the read — same language as the Map, shrunk down.

A (thread) and C (tucked) answer your bounce idea the right way — the orbs' distance carries meaning (close vs wide), so any motion is earned, not decorative. B (split orb) is the most compact and ties to the split history chips already in the app. D (stacked) trades the hero feel for zero ambiguity. Open question underneath all of them: does the widget always show Us when you're paired, or stay "you" by default and flip to Us only when Alex has checked in today? I'd lean the latter — your glance stays yours, and "together" appears when there's actually a together to show.
