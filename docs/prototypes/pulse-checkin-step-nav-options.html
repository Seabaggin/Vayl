Which one

OptionFeel
1 Solid fillClearest, most obviously "current" — closest to a standard pagination control
2 Accent ringLighter than a fill, still unambiguous, matches the same muted purple "Settings"/"Partner" already use
3 Spectrum fillStill the full cyan→purple→magenta gradient — kept as-is for comparison, but louder than option 2's single-tone purple
4 Size + glow onlyMost ambient/quiet — risks reading as less tappable at a glance
5 Dot markerNearly invisible until you look for it — safest if you want the nav to stay out of the way entirely

My lean is still 2 — clear enough at a glance, doesn't introduce a flat solid badge shape into a screen that's otherwise all glow/gradient/glass, and now it uses the same quiet label-purple already established by "Settings"/"Partner" instead of solo cyan or the louder full gradient.
