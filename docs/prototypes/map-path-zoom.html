Me · zoomed in

Big nodes, tight crop on you-are-here + next step. Your stance flags and notes are legible. Forward-leaning: "my next move and how I feel about it." Full screen zooms to your current phase with your annotations.

Us · zoomed out

Small nodes, the whole winding road, walked-together glowing, phases visible. Retrospective: "look how far we've come." Full screen is the entire 13-landmark trail with Mission Briefs.

Why this is the right differentiator: it is one map, two cameras, so it stays honest and cheap (same data). The zoom encodes the lens meaning directly, Me is close and personal and forward, Us is wide and shared and retrospective, and it stacks with the annotation layer you already have (stance flags on Me, clean shared trail on Us). One wording guardrail kept: the Me zoom centers on your position and next step, never "what you looked at" (that would drift toward tracking, off-limits).
