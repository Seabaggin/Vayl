Desire Map reveal · three telegraphed ceremonies

Same payoff in all three: each shared desire resolves from two seeds, your purple meeting their magenta, into one bright star. They differ only in the wind-up and how the constellation assembles. In the app the couple's ID picks one and it stays theirs, so a new partner or a friend gets a different one.

Gather
The field stills, light pulls to a point at center, the hero forms, then the rest radiate outward. The intimate one.
Sweep
A soft band passes across the field; each pair snaps together in its wake, left to right. The cinematic one.
Constellate
All the scattered seed pairs appear first, the held breath, then everything merges at once. The revelatory one.

How this maps to the build

One engine, three presets. The assembly reads a small config (telegraph, star order, line timing); the three presets fill it; a hash of the couple's ID picks 0, 1, or 2. Adding a fourth later is one struct.

Scope. The variant flavors the climactic full-sky assembly (post-unlock, or immediately for an already-Core couple). The intimate beat-1 free star stays constant across all three, so the free funnel and the quiet first touch do not change.

Reduce motion. All three collapse to the same dignified static end state, stars lit and lines connected, no movement.
